Bacon Risotto

Description

Creamy Risotto with crisp bacon.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups of Arborio Rice
  • 200g of Bacon, diced
  • 1 Tablespoon Unsalted Butter
  • 150mL White wine
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 600mL Broth
  • 70g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Half onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Optional: tomatoes.

Steps:

  1. First you need to cooked the bacon until it's crisp, when it's done reserve it;
  2. In the same pan you've cooked the bacon, cook the onion and the garlic in the bacon's fat, when the onions begin to turn transparent add the rice;
  3. Stir the rice until it starts to toast, then add the white wine, let it cook until it's absorbed;
  4. Now you start to add the broth, add just the right amount to cover the rice and keep stirring constantly, every time the broth is absorbed you add more to cover the rice, the proccess will take about to 15 minutes;
  5. When the rice is cooked, remove it from the heat and add the parmesan cheese and the butter, it's important that the butter is cold, stir it all together;
  6. Add the bacon and season it and season it to taste with salt and black pepper;
  7. Optionally add tomatoes and eat it, ITS DELICIOUS.
